About Me

I am a Senior GIS Analyst at Spekter GmbH with extensive experience in geospatial analysis, hydrodynamic modeling, and environmental research. My academic background includes a Master's in Geography and Environment from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, Bangladesh.

My research focuses on understanding and mitigating the impacts of water-related natural disasters and developing innovative geospatial tools for climate change adaptation and disaster management.
